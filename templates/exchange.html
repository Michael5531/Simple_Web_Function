Disability Support

Disability Services helps students who have a disability to access reasonable adjustments, and provides advice to prospective students about support services.

Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander student support

We provide admission pathways, assistance and support to all Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander students throughout their university journey. From admissions to graduation - including pastoral care, advocacy support, mentors and faculty staff, we can also provide referrals and tutorial assistance.

Careers Centre

Right from your first year at university, you have access to an abundance of career counselling services and resources to help put you in the best career position by the end of your degree.

Wellbeing

We have a range of services on campus such as doctor, dentist and counselling services. Others include health services, childcare information, faith and religion.

Learning Centre

We provide learning support to students at any stage, from your initial transition into university to postgraduate coursework and research.

Mathematics learning Centre

We provide support and guidance for eligible current and prospective students to develop their understanding, capacity and confidence in mathematics and statistics.
